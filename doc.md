## 4. üìä Add/Update Averages  
1. Go to **BULK OPERATION** section.  
2. Click on **Upload Averages**.  
3. **Download Sample File**.  
4. Open **Google Sheet** and search for *Fabric Average Sheet*.  
5. Click on **Pattern Average** tab.  
6. Copy data from **Row A3** or the averages you want to add/update.  
7. Paste the copied data into the downloaded sample file.  
8. Upload this file.
